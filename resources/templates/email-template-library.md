# Email Template Library

Ready-to-use cold email templates organized by signal type, persona level, and
sequence position. Adapt these to your ICP, offer, and proof.

## Signal-Triggered Templates

### Job Change (Champion Move)

**Email 1 (Days 14-21)**

Subject: [first_name] + [new_company]

Noticed you moved to [company] as [title]. When [similar_title] at [proof_company]
made a similar move, they needed [specific_outcome] in their first 90 days.

Built a short breakdown of how they did it. Worth sending over?

---

### Funding Announcement

**Email 1 (Week 1-2 Post-Announcement)**

Subject: [company] post-raise

Saw the [round_type] at [company]. Teams at this stage usually double
outbound volume within 6 months, but most of the infrastructure decisions
happen in weeks 2-6.

Put together a rundown of what [proof_company] locked in early. Want a copy?

---

### Hiring Signal (Role Posted)

**Email 1 (Within 2 Weeks)**

Subject: [open_role] at [company]

Saw the [open_role] posting. Companies hiring for this role usually hit
[pain_point] before the new hire ramps. [Proof_company] solved it by
[specific_approach] before their new [role] started.

Short version of how they set it up. Interested?

---

### Website Visitor (Identified)

**Email 1 (Within 24 Hours)**

Subject: [company] + [page_visited]

Noticed someone at [company] spent time on [page_topic]. Teams looking at
this usually care about [inferred_pain].

We helped [proof_company] with exactly that. Got a 2-minute breakdown if useful.

---

### Tech Stack Change

**Email 1 (Within 30 Days)**

Subject: [company] + [new_tool]

Saw [company] adopted [new_tool]. Teams switching to [tool] usually run
into [common_friction] in the first quarter.

[proof_company] avoided it by [approach]. Quick summary worth reading?

## Persona-Level Templates

### ATL (VP / C-Level / Director)

**First Touch**

Subject: [company] [strategic_signal]

[Signal reference, 5-12 words].
[P&L pain framing, 10-20 words].
[Outcome proof at peer scope, 10-15 words].

[Time-specific CTA, 5-10 words]?

**Follow-Up (Email 2, +3-4 Days)**

Subject: RE: [original_subject]

Different angle on the same idea.

[New value prop framing from a different direction].
[Second proof point or industry stat].

Still relevant, or off base?

---

### BTL (Manager / IC / Practitioner)

**First Touch**

Subject: [tool_or_process] at [company]

[Workflow signal reference, 5-15 words].
[Specific friction in their daily process, 15-25 words].
[Peer-level proof, 10-20 words].

[Resource-first CTA, 5-15 words].

**Follow-Up (Email 2, +3-5 Days)**

Subject: RE: [original_subject]

Quick follow-up with a different take.

[Second workflow pain or tool-specific angle].
[Template, guide, or benchmark as value offer].

Want me to send it over?

---

## Sequence Position Templates

### Email 2 (Add Context)

Switch the value proposition angle. If Email 1 led with saving money,
Email 2 leads with making money or saving time.

Same thread. No new subject line. No "just following up."

Structure: new angle (15-20 words) + second proof point (10-15 words) + softer CTA.

### Email 3 (Lower Friction)

Final touch. Accept the no. Make it easy to respond.

Structure: acknowledge silence (5-10 words) + one-line value reframe + binary close.

Example close patterns:
- "Worth a look, or not the right time?"
- "Should I stop reaching out?"
- "Not relevant, or just busy?"
